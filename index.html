About Tackathon

Join us for an exciting 48-hour hackathon where innovators, developers, and creators come together to build amazing solutions. Whether you're a seasoned developer or just starting out, Tackathon is the perfect platform to showcase your skills.

Problem Statements

1. Climate Change Solutions: Develop applications that help monitor and reduce carbon footprints.

2. Healthcare Innovations: Create tools to improve patient care and health monitoring.

3. Education Technology: Build platforms that enhance remote learning experiences.

4. Financial Inclusion: Design solutions to provide banking services to underserved communities.

5. Smart Cities: Innovate urban living with IoT and data-driven applications.
